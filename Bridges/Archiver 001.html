Archiver 001 instruction

1. Remove archiver lid with 4 screws
2. Remove disk drive bays (3)
3. Unscrew front chassis fans and remove front airfilter
4. Use wooden template to drill holes into black chassis
5. Deburr drilled holes remove sharp metal
6. Cut 7 inch Velcro strips
7. Drill small hole into end of Velcro strips
8.drill one hole in the bottom corner in back of chassis 
9. Rivet 4 Velcro sets onto chassis from underneath(8 rivets/8 washers)
10.install 78MLT backplate onto back of chassis
11.Mount hard drive SSD 250 on right side of black chassis with 2 screws
12.install motherboard onto chassis use 8 screws to fasten
13. Use gray ribbon cable to install pin configuration onto motherboard
14. Use 6300 CPU and fan set attach to motherboard
15. Make sure 6300 processor triangle matches placement onto motherboard
16. Attach CPU fan to motherboard 2 clips Fasten to motherboard, plug in fan
17.use power supply FS150LP archiver fasten to silver mesh with 2 screws in back of chassis
18.Install PCI Express cards (2)onto back of chassis with 1 screw for each card
19.Attach power data cable to SSD 250 HD
20.Attach black /yellow ATX to motherboard
21.plug in power to front 3 system fans from power supply
22.plug in 24 pin power connector to motherboard
23.Install system fan in back of chassis fasten with 2 screws
24.plug in system fan onto motherboard front left side
25.Install 1st set of 3 HD 4TB into right side front plug in Sata data cable,power supply Sata
26.Install 2nd set of 3 HD 4TB into front plug in Sata data cable,power supply Sata
27.Install 3rd set of 3 HD 4TB into left side of chassis plug in Sata data cable,power supply Sata
28.Install 4th set of 3 HD 4TB into left side of chassis plug in Sata data cable,power supply Sata
29.Zip tie any loose cables
