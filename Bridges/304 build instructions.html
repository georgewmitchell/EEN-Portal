304 building instructions
1. Remove 304 chassis from box
2. Unscrew rubber pegs(4) from chassis and remove bottom lid
3. Unscrew metal bracket (hard drive)from inside chassis 2 screws
4. Screw 750GB Hard drive to long side of metal bracket
5. Attach 4 GB RAM on to chassis line up slots and snap in
6. Slide 750 GB hard drive into slot and fasten metal bracket with 2 screws onto chassis
7.place lid and fasten 4 rubber button pegs
