301 Build Instructions

1.Install power supply (EN-PS-101) with 4 screws
2.Install system fan in front chassis with 2 screws
3.Install mother board back plate ENBP002 with 2 screws
4.Install back plate ENBP003 4 channel PCI card with 2 screws
5.Install 750 GB (EN-DC-100) hard drive with 4 screws( under chassis)
6.Install motherboard C847 with 2 back screws
7.Install 4 GB memory (EN-ME-100) stick push down until you hear a click
8.Install 16 V 4 amp fuse black/yellow (EN-CB-001) with 2 round hex screws tighten
9.Attach 4 channel DVR card (EN-DV-101) PCI riser card (EN-RC-100) left angle snap together
10.Attach PCI (EN-RC-100) / 4 ch. DVR card(EN-DV-101) onto motherboard push down firmly until it seats onto motherboard
11.Attach 24 pin power cable to motherboard
12.Attach black yellow ATX power cable to motherboard snap in firmly
13.Attach system fan to motherboard
14.Attach hard drive power cable to hard drive
15.Connect hard drive data cable from hard drive to motherboard
16.Connect black / yellow 16 V 4 amp fuse (EN-CB-105) to power supply
17.Zip tie all loose wires keep away from power supply if possible(follow cabling routing instructions)
