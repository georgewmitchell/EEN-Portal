620 build instructions

1.Use chassis EN CH 101
2.Remove Silver lid
3.Install risers screws onto chassis and tighten 
4.Remove Factory power led cables from front of chassis
5.Install front panel split power connector-EN-CB-102  (very tight) 
6.Attach comestic bracket from SuperMicro to front if chass 
7.Install Zotac back plate (EN-BP-009) with 2 screws
8.Install Zotac motherboard EN-MB-106 with 4 screws
9.Install 1 x 4GB RAM (EN-ME-100)
10.Install 4 x 4TB HD (EN-DC-102)
11.Attach power supply cable to back plane of chassis 
12.Plug-in system fan splitter (EN-CB-103)
13.Set pin configuration as follows
1.Power Led 
2.HDD Led 
3.Power SW 
4.Reset SW
14.Install I7 processor 4790 onto motherboard set in place match Triangle
15.Install CPU fan onto motherboard plug in fan to MB
16.Attach 24 pin power supply cable to motherboard
17.Attach ATX black yellow cable to motherboard (use extension cable)
18.Install for Sata data cables to backplane of chassis
19.Use zip ties for any loose wires to secure(follow cabling routing instructions)
20.Install Eagle eye 620 decal sticker to front of chassis
21.Attach Wan/Cam Lan sticker to back of chassis
